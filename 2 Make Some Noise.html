Make Some Noise

"Make some noise" means to "create a noisy sound" , or in other words, "to speak out your thoughts" , "don’t withhold your thoughts" and suchlike meanings. This phrase is usually referred to as "the act of giving voice and elucidating opinions in any form" , often used in the music scene.

This series has 13 pieces of jewellery that sound in a purely physical way, with electric guitar parts and vacuum tubes as the main materials, combined with pearl leather, etc. All the finished pieces can be used as percussion instruments to sound, and will sound with the shaking of the body.

I cooperated with musicians, engaged in sound design and actual performance, presented in the form of videos by using 13 jewellery works as percussion instruments. The initial stage features the emergence of a single piece of jewellery and sound, which is followed by a series of processes, finally giving rise to a resonance state featuring numerous pieces. Interspersed in the entire process are the performance state and dynamic details of the works. Moreover, in an effort to deliver the overall style of the work, upgrading filming technique and quick editing method are adopted. Ultimately, the work gives expression to a process in which players gradually sense and resonate with each other when making the sound, after which they collaborate in joint efforts to make the whole more enriched and harmonious with their own varied interpretations.

Music is often designed to be an event to awaken humanity, gathers the power to change society, the sound of jewellery has often been seen in antiquity as a symbol of wealth, status, or power. The combination of jewellery and musical Instruments has existed as early as the tribal period. Modern music originated from black music in Africa, among which rhythm is the most prominent feature. As the oldest musical instrument, percussion plays a very important role. The "noise" in music usually comes from percussion instruments.

Electric guitar is the product of modern science and technology and plays an indispensable role in modern music. As the core instrument of rock music, it symbolizes the self-consciousness of the youth and their reflection of and rebellion against the society. As the first batch of crucial components in the electronic industry, the vacuum tube has become the fundamental component of various inventions, ushering in the new world of electronic industry, unlocking the electronic information age. It is inseparably linked with the transmission of sound, and currently widely used in high-end power amplifier and audio equipment, such as guitar amp. Pearl, a material with distinct jewellery properties, was always deemed as a symbol of wealth, social status and power in the old days. However, over the prolonged course of history, it has been bestowed on a completely different symbolic meaning.
