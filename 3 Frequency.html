FREQUENCY

This project starts from sound to explore the relationship between man and frequency. In the first stage, my research is about cymatics, which is an interesting area between art and science. I tried to visualise different frequencies using powdered enamel and made an electronic device to achieve this before firing.

I found that using ground enamel with its sugar-like texture on a thin copper plate could complete the Chladni plate experiment. Within a certain range, the plate connected to the frequency generating device will resonate, producing regular geometric patterns.

While researching frequency, I gradually discovered that sound wave, vibration, frequency, harmonic resonance, overtone, musical note and various concepts or principles of mathematics, physics, biology, science, sound healing and even religion have a special and interesting relationship with each other.

This sketchbook shows these complex relationships that I have collected, analysed, summarised and collated. At the end of the sketchbook there is a sequence of frequencies that can create harmonic resonance with different parts of the human body. And I'm trying to use these frequencies to create a series of wearable instruments to heal the body and the mind.
